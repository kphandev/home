High School:
Ah, High School. The awkward stage in life where we start to wonder 'who the h*** do I want to be'. I wanted to be an engineer. In fact, the highlights of my time in adolescent purgatory were spent in Engineering--building robots, trebuchets, and questionable pyrotechnics.

University Pt.1:
Aw yeah! College, 'who the h*** do I want to be' part 2. I began college wanting to become an engineer but became fascinated by biological studies. My friends were doing it, the careers were hyped, it was all the rage! And so, I set off on my journey as a healthcare professional.

University Pt.2
There was a stage in my college career where I balanced classes, working 2 jobs, and volunteering. It was tough, I wanted to give up, but even through the struggle, it felt rewarding. This was a time where I really developed my work ethics, my philosophies, and marketability--I was ready for that big-boy job!

Graduation Adventure
Celebrate good times! My grueling trek through university had finally come to an end. I proceeded to use my last summer vacation as efficiently as possible: travelling, working part-time, and goofing off with friends. With my resume and experience, I'd get a job in no time...besides, how hard could it be?

Learn, adapt, build, repeat.
